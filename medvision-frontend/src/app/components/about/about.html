Empowering Precision Healthcare

At the intersection of medicine and machine learning lies MedVision. Our mission is to provide fast, accurate, and accessible preliminary diagnostic insights through AI.

We believe that medical technology should be both powerful and intuitive. MedVision combines state-of-the-art deep learning with a seamless Angular-based interface to transform complex data into actionable visual insights. By highlighting critical areas in X-ray imagery, we aim to reduce diagnostic latency and support better patient outcomes

On the Importance of "Explainability"

One of the greatest hurdles in clinical AI is the "black box" problem—the inability to see why a machine made a specific decision. MedVision solves this by integrating Explainable AI (XAI) at its core. Through the implementation of Grad-CAM algorithms, the platform doesn't just provide a percentage of probability; it provides a visual rationale. By overlaying heatmaps on original X-ray data, we bridge the gap between raw data and clinical intuition, allowing users to verify that the model is looking at the correct anatomical markers.
